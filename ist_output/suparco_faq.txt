SUPARCO (Pakistan Space and Upper Atmosphere Research Commission) is Pakistan's national space agency. IST collaborates with SUPARCO — e.g. ICUBE-Qamar lunar CubeSat (with SUPARCO and China's SJTU), ICUBE-1. ICAST unites IST's ICASE and SUPARCO's ICS. Graduates work at SUPARCO. (Note: SPARCO often refers to SUPARCO.)
