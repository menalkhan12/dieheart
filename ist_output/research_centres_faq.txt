IST Research Centres and Labs:
IST has multiple research centres and labs. Office of Research, Innovation and Commercialization (ORIC) oversees research at IST since 2011. Research Units include: IST Lunar CubeSat ICUBE-Q (lunar CubeSat, Chang'e 6 mission), CSET (Center for Surface Engineering and Tribology), Business Incubation Center (BIC), National Center for Failure Analysis (NCFA). Research Centres and Labs: Astronomy Resource Center (ARC), Space Systems Lab (SSL), Cyber and Information Security Lab (CISL), Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision Lab, Bio-signal processing and Computational NeuroScience lab (BiCoNeS), Wireless Signal Processing Lab (WiSP), Propulsion Lab, Aerodynamics Lab, Control Systems Lab, Center for Vibration Testing and Condition Monitoring, EMC Solutions. IST collaborates with SUPARCO, NCGSA, and international partners.
