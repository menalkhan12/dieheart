BS Biotechnology eligibility at IST: FSc (12 years of education) or an IBCC equivalent qualification in any science group, with at least one of the following subjects: Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Mathematics, or Computer Science. Minimum 33% in Entry Test acceptable for IST. FSC pre-engineering students have Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics — they qualify. Yes, FSC pre-engineering students can apply for BS Biotechnology.
