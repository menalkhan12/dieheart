Fee structure for BS programs at IST (Institute of Space Technology)

BS programs (including BS Computer Science, BS Avionics Engineering, BS Aerospace Engineering, BS Electrical Engineering, BS Mechanical Engineering, BS Space Science, and all other undergraduate programs) follow the same fee structure.

One-time charges at admission: Admission Fee Rs 35,000. Endowment Fund Rs 7,000. Library Development Fund Rs 7,000. Total one-time charges Rs 49,000 (local).

Per semester: Tuition Fee Rs 151,534. Service Charges Rs 8,470. Safe/Smart Campus Charges Rs 2,420. Total per semester Rs 162,424 (local). Tuition may vary slightly by program; the figures above are representative for BS programs.

Optional: Hostel dormitory Rs 55,000 per semester. Application processing fee (non-refundable) Rs 3,000. Fees are subject to change; confirm latest structure on IST website.

MS and PhD programs (including MS Computer Science, MS Aerospace, MS Electrical, etc.) share the same fee structure. One-time: Admission Rs 25,000, Endowment Rs 7,000, Library Development Rs 7,000, Total Rs 39,000. Per semester: Tuition Rs 80,526, Service Charges Rs 4,840, Safe/Smart Campus Rs 2,420, Total per semester Rs 87,786 (local).
